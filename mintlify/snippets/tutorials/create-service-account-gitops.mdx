1.  Log in as `Workspace Admin`, and go to **IAM & Admin** > **Users & Groups**. Click **+ Add User**, fill in with `api-sample`, choose the `Workspace Member` & `GitOps Service Agent` role sufficient for this tutorial and click **Confirm**.

1.  Find the newly created service account and **Copy Service Key**. We will use this token to authenticate the API calls.
